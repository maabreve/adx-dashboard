Compliance

Compliance is a Power BI Workspace with reports for tooling and patching compliance.

It provides a consolidated overview and detailed list of hosts not compliant in terms of tools (Crowdstrike, Defender, etc.) and patching.


(click on the image to open in a new tab)


Data Sources

Compliance relies on a variety of feeds to collect details about BP hosts (client and server computers) and patches available and applied to each host.


Data Refresh

Compliance model is refreshed daily 04:00 AM UTC.
