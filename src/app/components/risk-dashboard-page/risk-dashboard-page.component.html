Security Risk Dashboard

The Risk Dashboard application is a management dashboard providing insights into all areas of Cyber Risk within BP, highlighting areas where some action needs to be taken.

It provides CIOs with a consolidate picture of cyber security risk and related informationm, so that they are informed of their current cyber risk exposure to take corrective actions and prioritize resources accordingly.

Data Sources

Risk Dashboard relies on a variety of feeds processed from the SDS Data Lake:

  • SNOW Software Instance
  • Tooling Compliance (+ details)
  • IT4IT
  • Palantir Barometer/li>
  • Resilient Incidents
  • RiskRecon Suppliers
  • Saviynt
  • Unity

Data Refresh

Risk Dashboard is refreshed daily 04:30 AM UTC.
